Treat trouble shooting process as an application of the hypothetico-deductive method.

**The Step-by-Step Process:**
1. **Problem Report:** Acknowledge the initial alert or issue.
2. **Triage:** Assess the severity and impact. Consider re-triaging if necessary.
3. **Examine:** Gather relevant data and logs.
4. **Diagnose:** Formulate a hypothesis based on the data.
5. **Test/Treat:** Apply a fix or run a test to validate the hypothesis.

**Why this matters:**
This systematic approach helps avoid common troubleshooting pitfalls, such as:
* Looking at irrelevant metrics or misunderstanding what the metrics mean.
* Misunderstanding how the underlying system actually works.
* Coming up with improbable theories to fix the problem rather than following the data.
